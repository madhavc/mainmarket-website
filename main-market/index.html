Freshness, Quality, and Great Customer Service!

Come in for the best carne asada in town! We’re open daily from 8am to 8pm.

Main Market is a family-owned, local beer, wine, liqour, grocery and meat market (carniceria). Serving Whittier since 1999. Reminiscent of the classic Mexican-style butcher shop, we offer only the finest and freshest premium quality meat. Meat is cut to your liking and seasoned just right. Freshness, quality, and the best customer service in town is why our customers keep coming back. Come on in and we’ll make you a believer too!
